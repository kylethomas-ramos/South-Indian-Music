Games

Put your knowledge to the test with four interactive games rooted in Karnatak rhythmic theory.

🎧
Syllable Match
A solkaṭṭu phrase appears — can you identify which jāti it belongs to? Tests whether you've internalized the characteristic phrase shapes of tiśra, chatusra, khaṇḍa, miśra, and saṅkīrṇa.

Mōrā Builder
Given a phrase and a tāḷa, calculate the correct gap length so that the mōrā form — (statement)[gap](statement)[gap](statement) — fills an exact number of cycles and lands on sam. A math puzzle wrapped in rhythm.

🔍
Gopucca Puzzle
A mōrā plays with statements that shrink at each repetition — the gopucca (cow-tail) shape. Your task: identify the original phrase and the gap value from the pattern you observe.

Hand Gesture Guide
An interactive animated guide to every tāḷa's hand gesture sequence — claps, waves, and finger counts. Follow along in real time. Found on the Learn page.
